What does the company do, or offer?

The Kollective is a online store that features official goods from Kpop groups. The unlimited range of products and collaborations from the artists can be found here. Kollective does not offer services for ordering pre-sale items.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

It is a helper that hosts collection of products where fans can come to easily find their favorite groups' merchandise which may otherwise be difficult to look for on various online platforms.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

The store offers a category system where fans can simply breeze through the selection process by filtering only the products of their selected group.
